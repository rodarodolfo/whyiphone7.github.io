Why iPhone?

Why of all phones is iPhone? Well you might be asking why there are so many people who loves iPhone.

Few of the reasons are:

1) Apple has better hardware and software integration like the 3D Touch displays that Androids phones don't have. iPhone also has the unique camera feature which is called the Live photos, which captures video with audio during and after your shot. Other Android phones have similar features but according to users Live photos is easer to use.

2) its been said that iPhone is idiot proof (Ease of Use). Meaning it is the easiest phone to use as compared to other phones.

3) iPhone OS always have updates.

4) iPhone apps are first and better looking according to many online guides.

5) Works with all devices. If you have iPad and Macs, all your information flows smoothly from one device to the other. For example, you can sycn all photos to all your apple devices easily, can also answer phone call on youe iPad or Mac, and you can also send text messages from your ither devices. It also has the feature called Handoff that allows you to start a task on your iPhone and continue on your iPhone or your iPad or your Mac.

6) Resell value of iPhone is higher. If you sell your 1yr or 2yr old iPhone it is definitely worth more as compared to other phones.
